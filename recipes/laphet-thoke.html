Laphet Thoke Recipe

Description

Fresh vegetables tossed in Lahpet and arrays of toppings that give you an amazing texture, flavors, and taste. It’s hard to explain. You just have to try it to know what I mean!

Submitted by Marvellina

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons of fermented Laphet
  • ½ cup sliced tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of fried yellow peas
  • 2 tablespoons of fried garlic chips
  • 3 tablespoons of crushed roasted peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons of dried shrmip
  • 1 tablespoon of toasted sesame seeds
  • 1½ tablespoon of chopped green chili peppers
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put laphet in a bowl, mash them gently with a spoon and add a little oil and mix until soft and fragrant
  2. Add sliced tomatoes, penuts, fried peas, sesame seed and garlic chips
  3. Season with fish sauce, lemon juice and a pinch of salt
  4. Mix everything until fragrant
  5. Tast and adjust the seasoning if needed
  6. Serve with extra crunchy toppings and hot tea
